## üéØ TRANSLATION STRATEGY

**For Romance Languages (Spanish, French, Portuguese, Italian):**
- Focus on: Gender agreement, articles, prepositions, subjunctive moods
- Less worry: Cases (mostly gone!), capitalization

**For Germanic Languages (German):**
- Focus on: Case system (4 cases), case-dependent prepositions, capitalization
- Medium worry: Gender (3), prepositions change articles

**For Slavic Languages (Russian):**
- Focus on: Case system (6 cases!), aspect (perfective/imperfective), gender in past tense
- Medium worry: Prepositional cases, article-less structure

**For Agglutinative Languages (Turkish):**
- Focus on: Case suffixes, possessive suffixes, question particles
- Less worry: Capitalization, genders, most irregular patterns
